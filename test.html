User Story

A User Story is a thin slice of value, written from the user/customer's perspective, and is small enough to be completed by the team in a few days.

OKR

OKR, standing for Objectives and Key Results, involves actionable, memorable, and qualitative Objectives paired with measurable, time-bound Key Results. Combined, OKRs enable teams and organizations to enhance engagement, promote alignment, and monitor advancement towards strategic goals.

Feature

A Feature is functionality with quantifiable value for the customer or business that can be delivered by the team in a few weeks. The feature can be decomposed into user stories.

Strategy

A product strategy is a detailed plan outlining how a company's product vision will be achieved. It defines the roadmap for the product, spelling out the measures needed to meet the market demands, the competitive landscape, the target audience, and the unique value proposition.

Epic

An Epic is an initiative that will take months to deliver and can be decomposed into Features. The value will drive towards a strategic outcome.

Vision

A product Vision embodies the core purpose or "why" of the product. Representing a long-term target, it directs the product's path, defining what the company seeks to accomplish, the intended audience, and its distinctive niche in the market. An effective product vision energizes the product team and stakeholders by presenting a unified and inspiring trajectory for the product.
